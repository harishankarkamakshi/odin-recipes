Tomato Dal

Tomato pappu is a tangy lentil dish made with healthy pigeon pea lentils (tuvar dal), plenty of tomatoes, piquant onions, fragrant herbs and spices.Also known as tomato dal this recipe is inspired from the Andhra cuisine known for its bold and spicy flavors

Ingredients

  1. Tur Dal
  2. Tomatoes (6-8 small)
  3. oil (5 tsp)
  4. Rai (Musterd seeds)(1 tsp)
  5. RedChille Powder (Your chioce)(2 tsp recomended)
  6. Turmeric (1/2 tsp)
  7. Red Chillies (3)
  8. Hingh (Asefotida)(1/2 tsp)
  9. Curry Leaves (10 Leaves)
  10. coriander leaves (parsley)(1/4hand)

Procedure

  1. Heat oil in a heavy bottomed vessel, add mustard seeds, let them pop, add the cumin seeds, garlic, red chillis, and curry leaves and saute for few secs. Add the chopped onions, green chilies and ginger and sautÃ© till onions turn slightly pink.
  2. Add the chopped tomatoes and stir fry for 4-5 mts. Add the turmeric pwd and salt and combine.
  3. Add the cooked and mashed dal to the onion-tomato mixture. Add 1 cup water and cook for another 10 mts on low-medium heat. Garnish with coriander leaves.
  4. Serve with white rice or hot rotis.
